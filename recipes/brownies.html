Brownies

Brownies are a baked good that typically contain chocolate, although the ingredients are similar to what is used for chocolate cake a brownie is denser and heavier than a cake.

Different recipes may produce a fudgier brownie, depending on the ingredients or method of combination that is used

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

  2. Step 2

    In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

  3. Step 3

    Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
