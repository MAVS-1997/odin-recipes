Mashed potatotes

is a dish made by mashing boiled potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/2 cu shredded Asiago cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons whipped butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until fork-tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and place into a large bowl.

  2. Mash potatoes with a potato masher. Add Asiago cheese, milk, butter, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper; whisk until combined.
